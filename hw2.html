Time Management and Estimaation

Time management during a project is important. When a business asks for something to be implemented there are a lot of assumptions. When these assumptuions are made they should be documented and reviewed by both parties. These assumpitions can affect the amount of work that is being compleleted in a certain time period. For example, a certian implementaion may be reqiured in the project and it must be researched. The assumption that is made is the amount of time spent researching. Are the top five solutions researched or are all solutions tested in research? That is why there are specifics that are communitcated to prevent the assumptions.
